  • Sponsored meals for 11 schools in Kenya (2140 students) with No Hungry Children

This year, we aim to make ripples of positive change.

Every dollar dedicated to our impact fund will be directed

to an NGO partner of your choice.

$1 plants one mangrove in Haiti; empowering communities. (three mangroves sequester one metric ton of carbon)

Our event aims to be carbon negative by planting mangroves which sequester four to ten times more carbon than terrestrial plants.

Glimpse into the Freedom Healing Ranch

The Freedom Healing Ranch is a sanctuary that stands as a testament to transformation.

Once a hunting lodge, it is being lovingly revitalized into a healing sanctuary by the Carson Family.

Participants travel far and wide for deep inner work with different alternative medicines and modalities.

On nearly 2000 acres of land, they hold a vision to regenerate the land through permaculture and agroforestry, build out community living spaces, work sustainably with the Ogallala Aquifer and grow a community that can create waves of positive impact.
