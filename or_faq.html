Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the venue located?

These are the GPS coordinates of the gate are: 41.843027109740824, -96.32824292529764

We do not recommend mapping to this GPS point. Mapping apps often take people to the wrong place. Instead, we recommend navigating to Tekamah, Nebraska.

  1. Take Highway 32W out of Tekamah for 8 miles and turn right onto County Road 23, which is a blacktop road.
  2. Go north 3 miles to the third crossroad and turn right onto County Road L, which is a gravel road
  3. Follow the road for 1 mile until it ends at the driveway for the ranch

How can I get to the venue? (transportation)

The ranch is about an hour outside of Omaha, Nebraska. We highly recommend you use our shuttle service as there is limited parking and you won’t need a car during the festival.

What is the Cost and Schedule for the Shuttle Service?

Round trip tickets between OMA and the ranch are $80. Seats are available on a first come first serve basis – you don’t have a specific time. Closer to the event we will send out the final schedule for the shuttle service, but this is the preliminary schedule:

Is there a parking space?

Yes, there is parking space, but is limited and we recommend carpooling or taking the shuttle if you are flying in.

What are the available options for accommodation?

The three main options are Shiftpods (https://shiftpod.com/shiftpod/) with a Queen bed or with two twin beds. There are also canvas tents and authentic Mongolian yurts that were just brought over and built by Mongolian craftsmen.

Will there be power outlets in my tent?

Yes, there will be a 120V outlet available to you and lighting in your accommodation.

What Should I Bring?

We encourage you to bring a variety of clothes. This is normally a nice mild time of year, but nights can be chilly, and days can be warmer. In the month of October, the average high is 63 F and the average low is 39 F. It rarely snows, but rain is possible. It would be a good idea to bring rain protection.

Can I bring my own camping gear?

No, you are encouraged to only bring what you’d typically bring to a festival. You won’t need your own tent, sleeping bag, or large camping gear. You may bring small gear such as chairs and hammocks.

Can I park my RV and stay there?

Yes, we have 5 RV parking spaces that we are offering for $1,200 each, plus $750 for each person which covers all costs for the festival. However, there are no RV hookups. We will have bathrooms and showers available, but we cannot provide electricity or water.

What’s Included in my Ticket Price?

Your ticket is “all inclusive” which means it pays for the accommodation you choose along with admission to events and food throughout the festival. Excluded from your ticket price, there will be an elixir bar, gift shop, and art gallery.

Do I Need to Bring Cash or Credit Cards?

This is a cashless event. We will have an RFID system which will enable you to make purchases not included in your ticket. You will not be able to purchase anything directly with cash or credit cards. The exception to this is the art gallery where you may make purchases by wire or credit/debit card.

Can I bring my own food and beverages?

You may bring your own drinks and snacks, but there will be plenty of excellent food available to you.

Will alcohol be available at the event?

This is an alcohol-free event.

Are showers and restroom facilities provided?

Yes, we will have luxury trailer bathrooms available.

Is there a minimum age requirement?

The minimum age of admittance is 18 years old.

Can I attend only on specific days? Are day passes available?

Tickets cover the cost for the full event and there are no day passes. If you need to arrive late or leave the festival early, then you will need to arrange your own transportation.

Is the use of psychedelic substances permitted at the event? Will they be provided?

Psychedelic substances are illegal in the state of Nebraska. We will not be providing them for attendees.

What safety measures are in place?

We will have EMTs, first aid, and fire safety professionals on site and available throughout the event.

Are meals included in the event package?

Yes, your ticket price includes all food throughout the event. There will be an elixir bar available at an additional cost.

Will there be food and drink vendors?

No, there will not be any food or drink vendors.

Are there any restrictions on photography or recording during the event?

Yes, there are restrictions on photography and recording. Please read the event’s Terms and Conditions (sections 9 and 12).

List of items that are not allowed.

The Visitors are strictly prohibited from bringing any type of drugs, alcohol, illegal mind-altering substances, weapons, sharp and pointed objects, fireworks, flammable items, implement of destructions, speaker louder than 100 decibels, glass containers, animals (except for any Service Animal), drones and professional recording equipment’s at the festival.
